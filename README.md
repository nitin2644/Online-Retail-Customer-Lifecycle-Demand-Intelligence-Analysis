# Online Retail Customer Lifecycle & Demand Intelligence Analysis

End-to-end analytics on UCI Online Retail II dataset (~1M transactions).

**Tech Stack**  
Python (pandas, lifetimes, Prophet) • SQL (SQLite) • Looker Studio dashboards

**Analyses**  
- RFM segmentation  
- Cohort retention  
- Predictive CLV (BG/NBD + Gamma-Gamma)  
- Demand forecasting & Pareto revenue drivers

**Live Dashboard**  
👉 https://lookerstudio.google.com/reporting/5eeeda1c-c79e-41d6-91c8-d05dbf64529c/page/NCusF

**Full Notebook**  
👉 https://www.kaggle.com/code/iitm21f3002644/customer-lifecycle-demand-intelligence

**Key Insights**  
- Champions (14% of customers) generated 62% of £17.7M revenue  
- Retention: ~22% at 3 months, 18% at 6 months  
- Avg predicted CLV: £1,933 (median £815)  
- Top 20% products drove 79% revenue (Pareto confirmed)

**Business Value**  
Highlights £-impact opportunities via high-CLV retention & top-product focus.
